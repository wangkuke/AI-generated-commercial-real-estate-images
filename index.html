Frequently Asked Questions

Can I legally use AI-generated images for commercial listings? +

Yes, most paid tools like Midjourney (Pro) and Adobe Firefly provide commercial licenses. However, always check the specific license terms and disclose AI-generated images if required by your local MLS (Multiple Listing Service). Some platforms may have specific requirements about image authenticity.

Will AI replace professional real estate photographers? +

Not entirely. AI excels at pre-construction visualization, virtual staging, and concept images, while professional photography remains essential for high-end, finalized property assets and marketing materials. Many professionals now combine both: using AI for initial concepts and traditional photography for final listings.

How much does it cost to generate a commercial property image? +

Generally between $0.05 to $2 per image, compared to $300-$1000 for traditional photography. Costs vary by tool: Midjourney ($0.10-$0.50), DALL-E 3 ($0.04-$0.20), BoxBrownie ($1-$5 for virtual staging). Most tools offer subscription plans that lower per-image costs.

What are the limitations of AI-generated real estate images? +

Current limitations include: occasional inaccuracies in architectural details, challenges with consistent room dimensions, difficulty with specific branded furniture, and potential issues with photorealistic human figures. Always review AI-generated images for accuracy before using in marketing materials.
