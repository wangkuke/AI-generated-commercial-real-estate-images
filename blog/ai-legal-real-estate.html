AI Legal Guide for Real Estate 2025

Essential legal guidance for commercial real estate professionals using AI-generated property images, virtual staging, and architectural visualizations.

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

AI Legal Compliance Checklist

1

Copyright Ownership Verification Medium Risk

Verify AI tool terms of service for copyright ownership. Most AI-generated content may not have traditional copyright protection.

2

MLS Disclosure Requirements High Risk

Disclose AI-generated or virtually staged images in MLS listings. Check local MLS rules for specific disclosure requirements.

3

Truth in Advertising Compliance High Risk

Ensure AI-generated images accurately represent the property and don't mislead potential buyers or tenants.

4

Data Privacy Considerations Medium Risk

Review AI tool privacy policies, especially when uploading property photos containing personal information.
